Family Roots

Father: Alex Handley (c. 1892 – 5 Apr 1959, Little Rock, Pulaski County, Arkansas)

Mother: Jester Ann Pegross (Feb 1891 – Mar 1977, Tillar, Desha County, Arkansas)

Alex and Jester Ann settled in Jefferson County, Arkansas, where they raised David and his siblings in the Tillar community.

David grew up alongside five siblings. These brief profiles highlight the paths each of them took.

Alex Handley (c.1911–1938)

Born in Louisiana, Alex was the eldest son in the family.

He was recorded with the household in Jefferson County, Arkansas in 1930 and passed away in nearby Drew County on 29 July 1938.

Jimmie Handley (c.1914–1995)

Jimmie spent his early years in Louisiana before joining the family in Jefferson County, Arkansas.

He later made his home in Tillar and died in Saint Anne, Illinois in August 1995.

Mollie Handley (c.1917–1991)

Mollie was born in Louisiana and appeared in the family’s Jefferson County household in 1930.

By 1940 she was living in Winchester, Drew County, Arkansas, where she remained until her death in September 1991.

Joshua Handley (c.1918–1993)

Joshua also grew up in the Jefferson County household after his birth in Louisiana.

He later moved north and passed away in Chicago, Illinois on 6 March 1993.

Leola Handley (1921–2005)

Leola was born on 23 September 1921 and remained close to the family.

She was living in McGehee, Arkansas by the mid-1980s and died there on 9 June 2005.

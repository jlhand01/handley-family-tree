Life Story of Johnathan Handley

Early Life and Faith

Growing up as a little boy, I loved waking up early and journeying down those old dirt roads with my family to church. We would sit on the wooden benches, sing, and listen to the preacher deliver the Word.

I still remember the congregation singing “Amazing Grace, how sweet the sound.” Even today, I am deeply moved whenever I hear that great hymn.

Faith and Guiding Principles

As a child, I learned that I was receiving something that would last a lifetime: the understanding that the fear of God is the beginning of wisdom.

That truth has guided me throughout my life, and it remains the foundation of how I continue to live each day.

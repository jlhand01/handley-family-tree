Biography

Life Story of Solomon Handley

Born February 14, 1961

Early Life

I was born in Dermott, Arkansas, on February 14, 1961. I lived in Tillar for only six years, because on February 17th, just three days after my birthday, our family moved to Pine Bluff.

Because of this move, I did not have to work in the fields and miss school like many of my older brothers.

Education

In Pine Bluff, I attended Coleman School and later graduated from Watson Chapel High School in 1981.

Career

After graduating from high school, I worked at Jefferson Regional Medical Center, as well as at a Nursing Home and at NCTR in Jefferson, Arkansas.

Health

Up until 2014, I enjoyed good health and remained active. In April of that year, I was informed that I would need open-heart surgery. By the grace of God, I came through and, thankfully, I have not had any further health problems.

Faith and Ministry

I was recently ordained as a Deacon at Regenerated Missionary Baptist Church, where my pastor is my nephew, Rev. Levell Bass.

Children

No recorded children.

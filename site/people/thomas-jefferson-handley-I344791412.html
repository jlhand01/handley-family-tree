Thomas Handley

Life Story of Thomas J. Handley

Early Life

Thomas J. Handley grew up in Tillar, Arkansas, as the first-born son of David and Verna Handley. We grew up together as children, attending the same church and schools.

Love and Marriage

Thomas once told me that when he first saw me at just three weeks old, he knew I was the one he wanted to marry. He told his father, “I have met my wife.” His father replied that he would first have to go through my father.

We began dating when I was sixteen years old, and we were married when I turned eighteen and a half.

Together, we shared 43 years of marriage, raising six children and one grandson.

Family and Faith

Thomas was a wonderful man who made sure his family always had everything they needed. More importantly, he ensured that God was at the center of our lives.

For that, we love him deeply and miss him dearly.
